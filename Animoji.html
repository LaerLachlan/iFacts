Animoji

Apple also introduced a new feature on the iPhone X called Animoji. It is kind of like Snapchats and Instagrams Face Filters except for they are emojis. The iPhone scans your face while you are searching through emojis. Once you find one, whatever you do the emoji does. You can take a video of your Animoji and send it to friends through iMessage.
