IOS 11


Control Centre

The new IOS 11 Control Center will be amazing. It will be customizable and have way for options. Including Do not distrurb while driving, screen recording and an Apple TV Remote.

Notification Center

The new notification centre is completely redesigned. Now when you scroll from the top to the bottom it will bring up a lock screen like notification center. It will have a background (same as your lock screen background). It will have a clock. Plus all your notifications.

Siri

Siri has really improved. Siri now has a more human voice. It will sound more natural and less robot like. Siri has got a new awesome logo. Now Siri can also translate a few languages including French, Chinese and Spanish.

iPad

iPads with IOS 11 will now be even more like a computer. It will have a dock where you can keep your apps. Just like a Mac. Aswell as a new app switcher that will look more like a Mac's control center.
