Info

The new iPhones released in September of 2017 are looking pretty good. The iPhone 8, 8 Plus and iPhone X.

Price

Apple has released the price for all of these products. Surprisingly they are all under Us$1000! The iPhone 8's will be staring at US$699. While the iPhone X will be starting at US$999.

iPhone 8

The new iPhone 8 will be available in 64 GB or 256 GB. It will be waterproof and won't have a headphone jack just like the iPhone 7. Though it will be able to do wireless charging. There will be one facetime camera and one iSight camera.

iPhone 8 Plus

The iPhone 8 Plus will also be available in 64 GB and 256 GB. It will aslso be waterproof and able to do wireless charging. The phone will have one facetime camera but two iSight cameras just like the iPhone 7 Plus.

iPhone X

There is a lot of new stuff in the iPhone X. There is no home button. Yes that's right, no home button! Since there is no home button they have replaced Touch ID with something new, Face ID. Face ID scans your face instead of your finger in order to get into your phone. You must be wondering why they called it the iPhone X? It's because it was Apple's tenth anniversery. So instead of calling iPhone 9, they called it iPhone 10 or the roman numeral version of it, X.
